Our Story

140 years of uncompromising excellence

140 Years of Grandeur

Nestled amid the gentle whisper of history, The Grand Aurelion has stood as a monument to opulence since 1882. Every corridor breathes artistry; every chamber holds a story of kings and dreamers.

Our estate spans 12 acres of sculpted gardens, gilded ballrooms, and intimate sanctuaries designed for those who demand nothing short of perfection.

We do not merely offer accommodation — we offer an experience that redefines your sense of extraordinary. From the moment your car approaches our gates, every sense is engaged in a choreography of luxury.
